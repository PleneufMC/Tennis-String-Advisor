What is the RA index?
The RA (Rahmen-Analyzer) index measures racquet stiffness. The higher the number, the stiffer the racquet.

  • 50-60 : Very flexible (comfort++)
  • 60-65 : Flexible (balance)
  • 65-70 : Stiff (power+)
  • 70+ : Very stiff (power++)

You'll find this value on the racquet frame or in its technical documentation.
