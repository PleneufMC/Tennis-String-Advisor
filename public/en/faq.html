The RCS (Recommendation Comfort Score) is an indicator from 0 to 100 that evaluates joint stress of your tennis setup (racquet + string + tension).

  • Low score (0-30): Maximum comfort, ideal for sensitive arms
  • Medium score (30-60): Balance of comfort/performance
  • High score (60-100): Maximum performance, more joint stress

No. The RCS score is a decision-support tool based on technical data. It does not replace medical advice. In case of pain or injury (tennis elbow, etc.), consult a healthcare professional.

Tension depends on several factors:

  • Low (40-48 lbs / 18-22 kg): More power and comfort
  • Medium (48-55 lbs / 22-25 kg): Balance
  • High (55-65 lbs / 25-30 kg): More control
